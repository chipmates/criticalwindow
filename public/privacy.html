Privacy

The short version, which is also the whole version: this game collects nothing about you. No accounts, no tracking, no analytics, no cookies, no telemetry. These are design commitments, and because the game is open source, you can verify them in the code rather than take our word.

What stays on your device

Your saved runs and settings live in your browser's localStorage. They never leave your device; there is no server to send them to. Deleting your browser data for this site deletes them.

What the network sees

The game is a set of static files. When you load or play it, your browser requests those files, and the optional music and narration load the same way the first time you enable sound. Our host, Cloudflare Pages (Cloudflare, Inc.), serves these files and, like any web server, processes technical connection data (such as your IP address) to deliver them and keeps ordinary, short-lived server logs. We add no logging of our own, and the site sends a Content-Security-Policy that forbids requests to any other host. Legal basis for this technical processing: legitimate interest in delivering a website you requested (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

Sharing a run

A "challenge link" encodes only your seed, worldview and data version in the link itself. Nothing is uploaded; sharing it is you sending a piece of text.
