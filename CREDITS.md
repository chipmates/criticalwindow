# Credits

## Music

Both tracks by **Scott Buckley** ([scottbuckley.com.au](https://www.scottbuckley.com.au)),
licensed [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/), re-encoded for the web.
Thank you, Scott. His library is a gift to projects like this one.

- "The Long Dark" (playlist, first track)
- "I Walk With Ghosts" (playlist, second track)

The two tracks play as one continuous playlist everywhere in the game, in that order,
wrapping around. Earlier prototypes used "Simulacra" and "Aphelion" (also Scott's);
they were retired in v0.2 for a calmer bed under a thinking-heavy game.

## Narration

The voice layer is generated at build time (ElevenLabs), always from the game's own
displayed text. Two synthetic voices: a narrator for history (prologue, endings,
scheduled beats) and an advisor for the room (memos, incidents, wildcards). Timestamp
files ship beside every clip for future captions. No audio is generated or fetched at
runtime, and all voice is off by default.
